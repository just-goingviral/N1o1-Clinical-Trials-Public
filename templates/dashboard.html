N1O1 Research

The N1O1 product line is based on Dr. Nathan Bryan's pioneering research into nitric oxide therapies for cardiovascular health.

Our scientific approach focuses on enhancing bioavailability of nitrite which converts to NO in the body.

Key benefits of nitric oxide supplementation:

  • Improved endothelial function
  • Enhanced blood flow regulation through vasodilation
  • Reduced arterial stiffness in conditions of hypoxia
  • Improved exercise capacity by modulating cGMP pathways
  • Support for immune function with measurable changes in plasma nitrite levels

Our simulations model both pharmacokinetics and half-life of nitrite in the plasma using a multi-compartment model.
